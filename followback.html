Instagram Clean-Up

Follow the steps below to find out which accounts don't follow you back on your Instagram account:

Step 1: Visit Instagram Account Center

Step 2: Under "Account Settings" click "Your information and permissions"

Step 3: Select "Download your information" and then "Download or transfer your information"

Step 4: Select "Some of your information"

Step 5: Scroll down and select "Followers and following" under the "Connections section" and click "next".

Step 6: Select "Download to device"

Step 7: Select "All time", enter your personal email, select "JSON", and "low quality"

Step 8: Instagram will email the given email address from the previous step when the file are ready (1-5 mins)

Step 9: Download the zip file and input it below
